How to get your iCal URL:

  1. Open Google Calendar on your computer
  2. Click the gear icon (top right) → Settings
  3. On the left, click the calendar you want to share
  4. Scroll down to "Secret address in iCal format"
  5. Copy the URL — it starts with https://calendar.google.com/calendar/ical/

⚠ Do NOT paste your regular calendar page link — that won't work. It must be the secret iCal URL.
